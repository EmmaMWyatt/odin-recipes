Spaghetti Bolognese Kay Style

Have left Over meat from yesterday's roast? It's Pizza time!

A great way to use up leftovers! Lamb? Pork? Beef? Doesn't matter! Just make sure to add even more meat ontop of it

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 2ounces of water
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp dry yeast
  • 1tsp black pepper
  • 1tbsp oil
  • 500g bread flour

Toppings:

  • leftover roast meat
  • 2 cooked cumberland sausage
  • 1 tube of tomato puree
  • 1 pack of sandwich ham

Steps:

  1. mix together dough ingredients.
  2. when dough is formed. Roll it out flat to desired thickness.
  3. coat with tomato puree until it's evenly spread across the pizza.
  4. cut up sausages into slices and place on top.
  5. shred roast meat and sadwich ham and place on top.
  6. cook on high for 30mins.
  7. enjoy!
